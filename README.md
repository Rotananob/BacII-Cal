## 📖 Description

**BacII Calculator** (ម៉ាស៊ីនគណនាវិភាគលទ្ធផលប្រឡងបាក់ឌុប) is a free, offline-capable web application designed to help Cambodian Grade 12 students estimate and analyze their national Baccalaureate (Bac II) exam results — before the official announcement by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport (MoEYS).

The tool supports both major academic tracks:
- 🔬 **Science Track** (ថ្នាក់វិទ្យាសាស្ត្រ)
- 📚 **Social Science Track** (ថ្នាក់វិទ្យាសាស្ត្រសង្គម)

---

## ✨ Features

- **Score Entry by Subject** — Input scores for each subject with their corresponding coefficients (weights)
- **Automatic Calculation** — Instantly computes total score (out of 475) and overall grade
- **Dual Track Support** — Separate subject tables for Science and Social Science students
- **Personalized Results** — Enter your name for a personalized result message
- **User Profile** — Save your full name, school, grade, phone, and email locally
- **Clear / Reset** — One-click data clearing to start fresh
- **Offline-First** — Runs entirely in the browser; no internet required after page load
- **Mobile Friendly** — Responsive design, usable on Android & iOS via WebView

---

## 🖥️ How to Use

1. **Step 1 — Enter your name** in the username field for a personalized greeting.
2. **Step 2 — Select your track:** Science or Social Science.
3. **Step 3 — Enter your scores** for each subject in the provided table.
4. **Click "គណនាលទ្ធផល"** (Calculate Results) to see your total score, status, and grade.
5. Use **"Clear"** to reset all entered data.

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer:** Results from this calculator are for estimation purposes only. Official results are determined solely by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport of Cambodia.

## 🔒 Privacy & Data Policy

This app is built with **privacy-first** principles:

- ✅ All calculations happen **100% in your browser** (client-side only)
- ✅ **No data is sent to any external server or third party**
- ✅ User data (name & scores) is stored **temporarily in browser Local Storage** and cleared when the session ends
- ✅ The app **does not use** GPS, Camera, or Contacts
- ✅ Fully compliant on both Web and Mobile (WebView) environments
